Nika,
Unscripted

A portrait of a comeback that hasn't happened yet.

The Concept

Most climbing films are about the send. This isn't one of them.

Nika, Unscripted is a short-form documentary exploring the space between being a child prodigy and being an independent athlete in a foreign city. It’s about the noise of the past—the records, the war, the court cases—and the silence required to actually climb well again.

"People see the 9a on a scorecard and they think the story is finished. For me, that's where the weight started."

Visual Style

High-contrast 16mm textures mixed with raw digital 4K. We trade the sweeping drone shots of typical climbing media for tight, claustrophobic close-ups of chalk, skin, and breathing. It is intimate, percussive, and honest.
