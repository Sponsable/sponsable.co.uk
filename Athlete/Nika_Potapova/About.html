Ask Nika Answered monthly
What does a normal training day look like for you right now?
Morning session on the board, usually two hours. Afternoon I do something that isn't climbing — gym, run, just walk around Paris. I used to train until I couldn't move. I don't do that anymore.
Do you ever think about quitting?
Yes. And then I get on the wall and I remember why I started. It's not complicated. It just feels that way sometimes.
