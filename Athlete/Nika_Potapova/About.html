Before there were walls, there were trees. That's where Nika learned to climb. When a friend took her to a gym, she was asked after a single session if she wanted to move straight into the advanced group. ... more

She said yes.

Within her first year she climbed 8a. By thirteen, 8c. At fourteen she broke the women's Zlagboard endurance record — and nearly the men's. At sixteen she climbed 9a, becoming the 23rd woman in history to do so.

"I know I can climb much better than I am right now. I'll come back. Just wait for my comeback."
