How Sponsable Works

Athletes and creators join Sponsable to earn through affiliate marketing while building a Sponsorship Score that proves their value to brands.

Brands discover and collaborate with vetted creators who align with their requirements and deliver measurable results.

As creators grow, agencies can scout top performers, manage sponsorships, and track performance automatically — saving time and improving deal quality.

Whether independent or represented, every athlete builds a transparent, data-backed record of their commercial impact — making Sponsable the trusted pathway to real sponsorships.

Our Business Model

We believe in transparency. Our business model is built on partnership and aligned with the success of our entire community.

  1. Shared Affiliate Commissions: When a creator generates a sale through an affiliate link, we retain a small percentage of the total commission paid by the brand. We only make money when our creators make money.
  2. Brand & Agency Subscriptions: We charge brands and agencies a recurring (SaaS) subscription fee to access our platform, analytics, creator discovery tools, and campaign management features.
  3. Brand Deal Service Fees: When a sponsorship deal is secured and processed through our platform, we charge a service fee (a "take rate"). This fee is significantly lower than a traditional talent agency, reflecting our role as a technology platform, not a personal agent.

This model allows us to keep the core Sponsable platform free for creators.

Our Symbol, Our Vision

Our logo is a tern, and it was chosen for a specific reason. The Arctic Tern holds the record for the longest migration on Earth (from pole to pole… every year!). The species embodies endurance, precision, and drive.

We envision a world where every creator, from grassroots athletes to global icons, can embark on their own journey and use Sponsable as the engine to turn their influence into a meaningful career. We believe in a world where every brand can sponsor responsibly, with confidence and trust.

Our founder, Ben Rodriguez-Cobham, is an adventurer and athlete who experienced the challenges of sponsorship first-hand. His background in expeditions and brand partnerships inspired him to found Sponsable as a more transparent, data-driven, and responsible pathway to sponsorship for all.
