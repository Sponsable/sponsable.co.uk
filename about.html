Our Mission

To democratise sponsorship opportunities for creators of all sizes while helping brands invest responsibly in proven, brand-safe talent.

We believe sponsorships should be fair, transparent, and driven by real performance — not follower counts or empty promises. Sponsable exists to make that happen.

Our Vision

A world where every creator, from grassroots athletes to global icons, can turn authentic influence into meaningful opportunity — and where every brand can sponsor responsibly with confidence and impact.

What We Stand For

  • Responsibility — Ethical, transparent, and fair partnerships.
  • Performance — Real results measured through verified data.
  • Community — Supporting creators and brands who share the same values.

Meet the Founder

Ben Rodriguez-Cobham

Founder & CEO

Ben is an adventurer, athlete, and storyteller with first-hand experience of the sponsorship challenges creators face. His background in polar expeditions and brand partnerships inspired Sponsable’s mission to make the creator economy more transparent, fair, and human.

Why I started Sponsable

While preparing for an expedition, I struggled to secure sponsorships — not due to lack of effort, but because there was no clear way to prove my commercial value to brands.

That revealed a wider problem: thousands of athletes and outdoor creators drive real sales through affiliate marketing but can’t turn that success into lasting partnerships. Meanwhile, brands have no reliable way to find creators who are both effective and brand-safe.

Sponsable solves this by building a responsible ecosystem where creators can prove their value and brands can invest with confidence.
