How Sponsable Works — Step By Step

1. Apply

Submit a short application with social handles and key details. The fee covers manual review and verification work.

2. Verification

We verify sales, engagement authenticity, and ethical alignment. Verification combines automated checks with trained human review.

3. Sponsorship Score

Your Score combines three pillars:

  • Reach: Verified audience size and impressions.
  • Revenue: Proven sales performance via affiliate links.
  • Sponsorship Fit: Brand alignment, content quality and authenticity.

4. Campaign Access

Eligible creators discover campaigns matched to their score and values. Brands receive vetted, transparent partner options.
