The Challenge

Finding the right creators shouldn’t be this hard. Affiliate programs offer measurable sales — but little brand control. Influencer campaigns build awareness — but rarely prove ROI.

Brands spend hours vetting applications, checking metrics, and hoping partnerships won’t damage reputation. Misleading numbers, inconsistent messaging, and poor brand fit make influencer marketing risky and resource-heavy.

The Sponsable Solution

Sponsable unites affiliate performance and influencer authenticity — so you get measurable ROI and partnerships you can trust.

  • Track affiliate sales, engagement, and sponsorship performance in real time.
  • Save hours of manual vetting and protect your brand reputation.
