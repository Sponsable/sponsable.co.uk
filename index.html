Why I Built Sponsable

I built Sponsable after struggling to get sponsored for an expedition — not because I lacked effort, but because I lacked credibility with brands.

I knew affiliate marketing could make me money, but it always felt one-sided. I was generating sales for brands I believed in, yet never building a real relationship with them.

Sponsable was born from that frustration — to give creators a transparent, data-driven way to prove their value and turn affiliate success into genuine sponsorships.

— Ben, Founder of Sponsable
