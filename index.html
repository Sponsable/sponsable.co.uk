Everything you need to know before joining the Sponsable waitlist

What is an affiliate marketing platform and how does Sponsable benefit me?

An affiliate marketing platform connects creators with brands, allowing you to earn commissions by promoting products. Sponsable goes further by tracking your affiliate sales and using your performance and audience engagement to build a Sponsorship Score that helps you unlock sponsorship opportunities.

Do I pay a subscription to use Sponsable as an affiliate?

No — Sponsable requires a one-time fee of £4.99 to join. This fee covers the thorough vetting process every creator must pass before promoting.

Why is there a vetting process?

The vetting process ensures Sponsable stays a safe and trusted space for creators and brands by verifying audience authenticity, content alignment, and previous partnership quality.

How do I find offers that fit my values and audience?

Complete a short onboarding questionnaire and explore campaigns tailored to your niche. Brands can also reach out directly for sponsorships you can accept or decline.
