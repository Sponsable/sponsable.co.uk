Everything you need to know before joining the Sponsable waitlist

What is Sponsable and how does it benefit me?

Sponsable is a data-driven affiliate platform.

We provide two core functions:

  • Monetisation: We connect you with brands and provide the tools to earn affiliate commissions.
  • Data: We ingest and analyse your affiliate and social performance to build your personal "Sponsorship Score."

This score helps you secure high-paying brand sponsorships.

Do I pay a subscription to use Sponsable?

No. The core platform is free for creators.

Our business model is based on alignment. Your success is our success. We make money in three ways:

  • We retain a small percentage of affiliate commissions.
  • We charge brands a SaaS fee to access our analytics.
  • We charge a small service fee on brand deals we facilitate.

You can read the full breakdown on our About page.

Why is there a vetting process?

Our platform's core asset is the integrity of its data and the quality of its network. The vetting process is a two-way filter:

  • It ensures our brand partners are accessing an authentic, high-quality creator base, not bots.
  • It protects you from being on a platform saturated with low-quality accounts, which devalues your presence and the opportunities you receive.
How do I find offers that fit my values?

During onboarding, we ask about your niche, audience, and values. Our engine then matches this data against brand campaigns, ensuring the opportunities you see are a high-probability fit for your audience.

What are the requirements to join?

    We evaluate creators on both qualitative and quantitative data. While there is no simple "follower minimum," we assess every applicant on three core metrics:

  • Audience Authenticity: e.g., high engagement rates, low bot scores.
  • Content Quality: a consistent and high-standard feed.
  • Brand Suitability: a professional approach to content and partnerships.
