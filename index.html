What is an affiliate marketing platform and how does Sponsable benefit me?

Sponsable connects creators with brands so you can earn commissions and build a Sponsorship Score that proves your value to brands for long-term partnerships.

No. Sponsable has a one-time £4.99 vetting fee — ensuring creators are authentic and brands are protected.

Why is there a vetting process?

To maintain a safe and high-quality space for brands and creators, ensuring only trusted, authentic influencers join.

How do I find offers that fit my values?

We tailor your recommendations based on your content and niche — and you can browse or favorite specific brands.

What are the requirements to join?

  • Alignment: Content aligns with brand ethics.
  • Professionalism: Ongoing partnership quality checks.
